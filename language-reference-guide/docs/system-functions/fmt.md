



<h1 class="heading"><span class="name">Format</span><span class="command">⎕FMT</span></h1>


##### Monadic `⎕FMT` means


[Display Form](format-monadic.md)
```apl
      ⎕FMT 2 4⍴⍳8
1 2 3 4
5 6 7 8

```

##### Dyadic `⎕FMT` means


[Format](format-dyadic.md)
```apl

  5 6.00  7 8.00
```
